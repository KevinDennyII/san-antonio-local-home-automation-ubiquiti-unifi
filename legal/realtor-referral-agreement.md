## 1. Purpose

Partner introduces Provider to buyers, sellers, and homeowners of luxury residential properties for UniFi networking, Home Assistant, and related private-AI estate services. This is a **channel / referral** relationship, not an equity partnership unless a separate agreement says otherwise.

## 2. Client ownership

| Relationship | Owner |
|--------------|--------|
| Real-estate transaction and agent–client relationship | Partner |
| Technical design, install, credentials, Concierge support, and post-sale tech relationship | Provider |
| Joint marketing assets co-branded under this agreement | Shared use per Section 6 |

Provider may contact referred clients for surveys, proposals, installs, and ongoing Concierge service. Partner remains the client’s realtor of record for real-estate matters.

## 3. Qualified referral

A **Qualified Referral** is a lead that:

1. Comes from Partner (intro email, text, or in-person warm handoff); and
2. Results in a signed Provider Statement of Work (SOW) and deposit within **180 days** of first intro; and
3. Is not already an active Provider prospect (Provider must disclose within 5 business days if conflict).

Site surveys paid by the homeowner count toward engagement but **referral fee is due only on closed install SOW** (or as amended in Schedule A).

## 4. Referral fee

**Default (Schedule A may override):**

| Fee type | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Install packages (A / B / C) | **10%** of Provider’s **net project fee** (hardware + labor + cabling charged to client, excluding sales tax, permits paid to third parties, and Concierge prepaid months) |
| Concierge retainer (first 12 months) | **0%** (Provider retains; optional: 5% of first 3 months if Partner prefers — set in Schedule A) |
| Pilot / discounted installs | Fee on **actual amount collected**, not list price |

**Payment timing:** Within **15 business days** after Provider receives the client milestone payment that makes the project ≥50% paid (or final payment if project is smaller). Provider sends Partner a remittance summary per closed job.

**Cap / floor (optional):** Minimum fee $[X] / maximum fee $[Y] per project — fill if desired.

## 5. Roles and presentation

- Partner may leave Provider rate cards and one-pagers after showings and at listing/buyer consultations.
- Provider is introduced as the **estate network & private AI specialist**, not as Partner’s employee.
- Only Provider quotes technical scope, timelines, and final pricing.
- Partner does not configure networks, Home Assistant, or credentials.

## 6. Joint marketing

Allowed with mutual written approval (email sufficient):

- Co-branded one-pagers and case studies (client permission required for photos/names)
- Short demo videos and social posts tagging both brands
- Partner website / listing materials linking to Provider

Not allowed without written approval: using the other’s trademarks in paid ads, implying employment or equity partnership, or sharing client PII beyond what is needed for the referral.

## 7. Exclusivity

**Non-exclusive** unless Schedule A states otherwise. Partner may refer other vendors; Provider may work with other agents and brokerages.

## 8. Confidentiality and data

Each party keeps confidential: client contact lists, pricing, and technical designs. Provider holds UniFi/HA admin credentials per its security defaults; Partner is not given admin access unless the homeowner requests it in writing.

## 9. Insurance and compliance

Provider maintains general liability and professional liability suitable for residential low-voltage / IT installs. Partner maintains licenses required for real-estate brokerage. Alarm monitoring and life-safety permitting remain with licensed alarm vendors unless Provider obtains required licenses.

## 10. Term and termination

Term: **12 months**, auto-renewing for 12-month periods unless either party gives **30 days** written notice. Termination does not cancel referral fees already earned on Qualified Referrals introduced before termination and closing within the 180-day window.

## 11. Independent contractors

Nothing herein creates a partnership, joint venture, or employment. Each party pays its own taxes and expenses.

## 12. Governing law

State of Texas. Venue: Bexar County, unless counsel advises otherwise.
