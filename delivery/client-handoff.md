## 6. What not to do

- Don’t factory-reset the Dream Machine without calling us  
- Don’t move IoT devices onto the main Estate SSID “to make them work”  
- Don’t port-forward Home Assistant on your router  
- Don’t share admin passwords with temporary contractors — ask us for a guest tech window
